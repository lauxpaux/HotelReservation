EXPLORE

Woodbury Forest is a real life enchanted forest full of beauty and wonders. It is home to a wide arrange of floura and founas, and one of the most diverse ecosystems in the northern hemisphere. Enjoy a guided tour of the locations used on films like The Whobit, Harry Photter, and Twilnight. If you would like to explore the forest on your own, you are welcomed to do that as well!

We strongly recommend that you are careful while on your tour as the forest can change shapes and become really confusing. I lost my favorite mug there one time, and a bottle of rum. Never found it again.
